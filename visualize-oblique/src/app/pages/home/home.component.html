About LINDAS

LINDAS (Linked Data Service) is the Swiss Federal platform for publishing and consuming Linked Open Data. It provides a SPARQL endpoint for querying RDF data cubes from various Swiss federal offices and organizations.

This visualizer provides an intuitive interface for exploring LINDAS data, creating visualizations, and building custom queries without requiring deep knowledge of SPARQL.
